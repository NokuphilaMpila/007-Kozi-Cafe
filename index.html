About Us

Welcome to 007 Kozi Cafe, located in the heart of Eldoraigne, Centurion. Our cafe offers a warm and welcoming atmosphere, perfect for catching up with friends or enjoying a quiet meal alone.

At 007 Kozi Cafe, we pride ourselves on our delicious food and fast, efficient service. Whether you’re looking for a quick takeaway meal or a leisurely dine-in experience, we’ve got you covered. Plus, we’re open 24/7, making us the ideal spot for late-night snacks or early morning coffee runs.

Our menu features a variety of options, from classic breakfast dishes to tasty small plates and decadent desserts. And with offerings like coffee, late-night food, and small plates, there’s something for everyone to enjoy.We can’t wait to welcome you!
